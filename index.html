Our Heritage

Three generations of tailoring.

Waqar began in a small atelier in the heart of Amman — a single tailor, a single bolt of cloth, and an unwavering belief that a man's thobe should be more than a garment. It should be a story.

"الأناقة لا تُشترى، بل تُنسج خيطاً خيطاً."

Today we still cut and finish each piece in our own workshops, using fabrics sourced from Italy, Japan and the Levant. No two thobes are quite the same — and that is precisely the point.
